## 1. Overview

RideWave ("**we**", "**us**", "**our**", or "**Company**") operates the RideWave mobile application and web services (the "**Service**"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information.

## 2. Personal Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide

- **Account Registration:** name, email, phone number, password, payment methods
- **Profile:** address, emergency contacts, driver license (for drivers), vehicle details
- **Transactions:** payment history, trip receipts, rating and feedback
- **Communications:** support messages, complaints, feedback

### B. Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates during trips (with your consent)
- **Device Information:** device ID, OS, app version, IP address
- **Usage Data:** trip history, app interactions, performance metrics
- **Cookies:** session tokens, analytics

### C. Third-Party Data

- **Background Checks:** driver verification data from background check providers
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe (for PCI-compliant payment processing)
- **Maps APIs:** Google Maps or Mapbox for location services

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Providing and improving the Service
- Processing payments securely via Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)
- Communicating with you (support, promos, updates)
- Safety and fraud prevention
- Legal compliance and dispute resolution
- Analytics and business intelligence (anonymized)

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not** sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

- **Service Providers:** payment processors, cloud hosting (AWS), analytics (Sentry)
- **Legal Requirements:** if compelled by law or court order
- **Safety:** if necessary to prevent harm or violation of terms

**Driver-Rider Masking:** Phone numbers are masked using a proxy service (Twilio) to protect privacy.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Users:** Personal data retained while account is active + 12 months after deletion
- **Trips:** 7 years for tax/regulatory compliance
- **Payments:** 7 years for financial records
- **Deleted User Data:** Purged within 30 days (per GDPR "Right to be Forgotten")

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

### GDPR (EU residents)
- Access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, portability
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge complaints with data protection authorities

### CCPA (CA residents)
- Right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

**To exercise rights:** Contact support@ridewave.com

## 7. Security

- HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit
- Encryption for sensitive fields at rest (passwords, payment tokens)
- PCI-DSS scope minimized via Stripe (no card data stored locally)
- Access controls and audit logging
- Regular security audits

**No method is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. International Data Transfers

If you are outside the US, your data may be transferred and processed in the US or other jurisdictions. By using RideWave, you consent to such transfers.

## 9. Children

RideWave is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
